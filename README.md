🤖 AI_BUTLER: Your Personal Royal Jarvis
Welcome to the AI_BUTLER repository! This project is a powerful, intelligent personal assistant designed to streamline your daily tasks, automate workflows, and act as your very own Royal Jarvis.

🚀 Key Features
Intelligent Planning: Built-in planner_agent to help you stay organized and manage your time effectively.

Voice-Powered: Integrated voice_engine for a seamless, hands-free interaction experience.

Adaptive Memory: Persistent memory.json handling to keep track of your important tasks and context.

Versatile Toolkit: A modular tools.py structure that allows for infinite scaling and feature additions.

🛠️ Built With
Python - The core engine driving the intelligence.

Generative AI - Smart processing for smarter responses.

💡 Why "Royal Jarvis"?
Just like Tony Stark's iconic assistant, AI_BUTLER is built to be proactive, efficient, and always ready to assist. Whether it’s managing study notes, automating scripts, or handling complex tasks, your digital butler is on the job.
